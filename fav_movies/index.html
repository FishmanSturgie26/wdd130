Favorite Movies

:Interstellar

2014

Interstellar is a science-fiction epic that explores space travel, time dilation, and the emotional bonds between parents and children. Its blend of hard science and emotional storytelling makes it both thought-provoking and visually stunning.

:Ratatouille

2007

Ratatouille tells the story of a rat who dreams of becoming a chef in Paris. The film celebrates creativity, passion, and the idea that greatness can come from unexpected places.

:No Country for Old Men

2007

No Country for Old Men is a tense crime thriller that examines fate, morality, and violence in the modern world. Its quiet storytelling and unforgettable antagonist create constant unease and suspense.

:Knives Out>

2019

Knives Out is a modern whodunit that blends classic mystery tropes with sharp humor and social commentary. Its clever structure and strong ensemble cast keep the story engaging from start to finish.

Perfect Blue

1997

Perfect Blue is a psychological thriller that explores identity, fame, and obsession. Its unsettling atmosphere and unreliable reality blur the line between fantasy and truth, leaving a lasting impact.
